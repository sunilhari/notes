This is the first post on my personal blog. I'm going to use this space to document things I study and learn — programming, computer science, tools, and anything else worth writing down.

## Why write?

Writing forces clarity. When I have to explain something in plain text, gaps in my understanding become obvious. Notes that live only in my head tend to fade. Writing them down makes them last.

## What to expect

Posts here will mostly be:

- **Notes on topics I'm studying** — language features, algorithms, concepts
- **How-to guides** — things I figured out and want to remember
- **Book/course notes** — summaries of things I'm reading or watching

## How this site works

Posts are written in Markdown (`.md` files) and organized with tags. Browse everything on the [Blog](/blog/) page or filter by topic on the [Tags](/tags/) page.
